Philosophy & Personal Life

Despite her success, Elizabeth remains grounded. She credits her upbringing for her resilience and humility and she continues to value family as the core of her identity. Outside of buisness, she is passionate about Philanthropy, supporting educational initiatives for young women in Malta who aspire to careers in buisness and finance.
She also enjoys travel, art and architecture, often drawing inspiration from global cities to bring fresh ideas back to Malta. Her friends describe her as driven, elegant and forwarding-thinking-a woman who embraces both legacy and innovation.
