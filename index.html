Elizabeth Morgan Merlin

Elizabeth Morgan Merlin (born December 18, 1993) is a Maltese enterpreneur and a millionaira buisnesswoman known for her strong ties to Malta's booming real estate sector. She built her wealth through strategic owernship of shares in her uncle's renowned db Group, one of the country's leading property and hospitality companies. With a sharp buisness mind and modern outlook, Elizabeth represents a new generation of Maltese investors, combining family legacy with her own drive to expand opportunities in real estate and beyond. She is the largest shareholder of db Group. Her family and their affliates own approximately 40 percent of db Group shares, which was founded by her uncle, Silvio Debono from Mellieha.
