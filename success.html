Career & Success

After completing her studies, Elizabeth returned to Malta with a renewed vision. Instead of starting entirely from scratch, she strategically positioned herself by acquiring shares in db Group, leveraging both her education and family connections. This move not only secured financial independence but also made her one of the younger millionaires in Maltese buisness landscape.

Within db Group, Elizabeth focused on innovation and modernization. She championed projects that emphasized sustainable building practices, luxury hospitalilty and smart investment models. Her role evolved beyond being a shareholder; she became a voice for young investors who wanted to merge tradition with modernity in Malta's rapidly expanding economy.
